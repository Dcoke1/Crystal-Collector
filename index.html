Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game. "Number to Hit"
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win by matching your total score to the random number "Number to Hit"
  • You lose the game if your total score goes over "Number to Hit"
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click the crystal
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
